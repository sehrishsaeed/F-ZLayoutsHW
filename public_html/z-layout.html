    About Images

    Types of image formats

    • PNG - Portable Network Graphic
      • Greater than 256 colors (16.7 million)
      • Supports Layer
      • Can be small, it supports compression but not the smallest for full color image
      • Scaling may or may not result in a loss of quality depending on the type of image and how it was saved
      • Supports transparency with a full color image
    • SVG - Scalable Vector Graphic
      • Basically math as a graphics format so graphics are made using formulas and coordinates
      • super small
      • Can be scaled without loss of quality
      • Used a lot in mobile apps because they're resolution independent because the math of the graphic renders on the device
    • GIF - Graphics Interchange Format
      • Only supports 256 colors
      • Supports animation
      • Does not support Transperency
      • Used with line art like cartoon characters
      • small files size but can be terrible quality for photos or anything other than line art
    • JPG - Joint Photographic Experts Group
      • Supports 16.7 Million Colors
      • Offers file compression
      • Best for photos and controlling the quality of a photo for use online
